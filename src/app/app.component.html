ENERGY MONITORING SYSTEM

In the modern world, highly protected electrical systems that combine a wide variety of electrically operated equipment still have some difficulties not only with monitoring and protecting such complex systems of the devices connected to the power supply during unstable energy but also with identifying the causes of the damage and eliminating the problem. As a result, it is common for several electrical devices or the entire system to be damaged. Energy Monitoring System facilitates the monitoring and analyzing the energy in such systems.

How It Works

Our product enables us to measure and monitor current, voltage, frequency, and power through the system. In order to collect the real-time data on energy usage, we use corresponding sensors.

The information received from sensors is sent to IoT server through WiFi using the ESP32 module. The data is processed by the specific algorithms and stored in database. We have selected a Influxdb database that is designed specifically for the time-series data.

The processed data should finally be visualized nicely. To do this, we use a web application - Grafana that allows to visualize collected information with charts, diagrams, dashboards, and other appropriate tools so that all information becomes easily perceptible for anyone.
